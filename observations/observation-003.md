# Observation 003

## Failure Type
Authority Mismatch

## Document Type
Healthcare Power of Attorney

## Scenario
A healthcare POA named a single adult child as the decision maker. However, during a hospitalization multiple siblings believed they shared decision authority.

## Discovery Moment
Care planning discussion with hospital staff.

## Resolution Required
Staff reviewed the document and clarified that only the named agent held legal authority.

## Operational Impact
Family conflict created delays in communication with the care team.

## Notes
Authority mismatches often arise when documents were completed years earlier and family expectations have changed.
