# Observation 002

## Failure Type
Execution Failure

## Document Type
Advance Healthcare Directive

## Scenario
A directive was presented during a medical event but was missing required witness signatures.

## Discovery Moment
Hospital intake.

## Resolution Required
The document could not be accepted as valid and a new directive was required.

## Operational Impact
Medical staff requested clarification of decision authority while the family worked to complete new paperwork.

## Notes
Execution errors often occur when documents are downloaded and filled out without confirming witnessing requirements.
