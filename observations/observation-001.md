# Observation 001

## Failure Type
Accessibility Failure

## Document Type
Healthcare Power of Attorney

## Scenario
Family reports that a healthcare power of attorney document exists and was completed with an attorney several years earlier. However, no copy could be located during a hospital admission.

## Discovery Moment
Emergency hospitalization.

## Resolution Required
Family attempted to locate copies through home files and contacted the attorney's office.

## Operational Impact
Hospital staff were temporarily unable to confirm the authorized decision maker, delaying some care decisions.

## Notes
This type of failure often occurs when documents are stored with attorneys or in physical files that are not accessible during medical events.
